BNAIC 2017

BNAIC is the Annual Benelux Conference on Artificial Intelligence. This year, the 29th edition of BNAIC is organized by the University of Groningen, under the auspices of the Benelux Association for Artificial Intelligence (BNVKI) and the Dutch Research School for Information and Knowledge Systems (SIKS).

BNAIC 2017 is held in Het Kasteel, Melkweg 1, Groningen, The Netherlands, on Wednesday November 8 and Thursday November 9, 2017. BNAIC 2017 will include invited speakers, research presentations, posters and demonstrations. Authors are invited to submit papers on all aspects of Artificial Intelligence.
